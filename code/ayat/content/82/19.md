### يَوْمَ لَا تَمْلِكُ نَفْسٌ لِنَفْسٍ شَيْئًا ۖ وَالْأَمْرُ يَوْمَئِذٍ لِلَّهِ
### meaning: 
It is a day when no soul will be of any avail to another soul and all command that day will belong to Allah.
