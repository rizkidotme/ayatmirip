أَمْ لَهُمْ إِلَٰهٌ غَيْرُ اللَّهِ ۚ سُبْحَانَ اللَّهِ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ
### meaning: 
Do they have any god other than Allah? Clear is Allah of any partners that they may ascribe [to Him]!
