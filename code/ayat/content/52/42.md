أَمْ يُرِيدُونَ كَيْدًا ۖ فَالَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا هُمُ الْمَكِيدُونَ
### meaning: 
Do they seek to outmaneuver [Allah]? But it is the faithless who are the outmaneuvered ones!
