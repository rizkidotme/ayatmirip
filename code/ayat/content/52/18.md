### فَاكِهِينَ بِمَا آتَاهُمْ رَبُّهُمْ وَوَقَاهُمْ رَبُّهُمْ عَذَابَ الْجَحِيمِ
### meaning: 
rejoicing because of what their Lord has given them, and that their Lord has saved them from the punishment of hell.
