وَمَا نَقَمُوا مِنْهُمْ إِلَّا أَنْ يُؤْمِنُوا بِاللَّهِ الْعَزِيزِ الْحَمِيدِ
### meaning: 
They were vindictive towards them only because they had faith in Allah, the All-mighty and the All-laudable,
