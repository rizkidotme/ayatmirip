أَوْ يَأْخُذَهُمْ عَلَىٰ تَخَوُّفٍ فَإِنَّ رَبَّكُمْ لَرَءُوفٌ رَحِيمٌ
### meaning: 
Or that He will not visit them with attrition? Indeed your Lord is most kind and merciful.
