خَلَقَ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضَ بِالْحَقِّ ۚ تَعَالَىٰ عَمَّا يُشْرِكُونَ
### meaning: 
He created the heavens and the earth with consummate wisdom. He is above having any partners that they ascribe [to Him].
