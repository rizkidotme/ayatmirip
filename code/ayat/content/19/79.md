كَلَّا ۚ سَنَكْتُبُ مَا يَقُولُ وَنَمُدُّ لَهُ مِنَ الْعَذَابِ مَدًّا
### meaning: 
No indeed! We will write down what he says, and We will prolong his punishment endlessly.
