### قَالَتْ أَنَّىٰ يَكُونُ لِي غُلَامٌ وَلَمْ يَمْسَسْنِي بَشَرٌ وَلَمْ أَكُ بَغِيًّا
### meaning: 
She said, ‘How shall I have a child seeing that no human being has ever touched me, nor have I been unchaste?’
