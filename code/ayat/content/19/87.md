### لَا يَمْلِكُونَ الشَّفَاعَةَ إِلَّا مَنِ اتَّخَذَ عِنْدَ الرَّحْمَٰنِ عَهْدًا
### meaning: 
no one will have the power to intercede [with Allah], except for him who has taken a covenant with the All-beneficent.
