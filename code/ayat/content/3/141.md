### وَلِيُمَحِّصَ اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَيَمْحَقَ الْكَافِرِينَ
### meaning: 
And so that Allah may purge [the hearts of] those who have faith and that He may wipe out the faithless.
