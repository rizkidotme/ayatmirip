وَمَكَرُوا وَمَكَرَ اللَّهُ ۖ وَاللَّهُ خَيْرُ الْمَاكِرِينَ
### meaning: 
Then they plotted [against Jesus], and Allah also devised, and Allah is the best of devisers.
