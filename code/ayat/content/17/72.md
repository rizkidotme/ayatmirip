### وَمَنْ كَانَ فِي هَٰذِهِ أَعْمَىٰ فَهُوَ فِي الْآخِرَةِ أَعْمَىٰ وَأَضَلُّ سَبِيلًا
### meaning: 
But whoever has been blind in this [world], will be blind in the Hereafter, and [even] more astray from the [right] way.
