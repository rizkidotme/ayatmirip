ذُرِّيَّةَ مَنْ حَمَلْنَا مَعَ نُوحٍ ۚ إِنَّهُ كَانَ عَبْدًا شَكُورًا
### meaning: 
descendants of those whom We carried [in the ark] with Noah. Indeed, he was a grateful servant.
