### رِزْقًا لِلْعِبَادِ ۖ وَأَحْيَيْنَا بِهِ بَلْدَةً مَيْتًا ۚ كَذَٰلِكَ الْخُرُوجُ
### meaning: 
as a provision for Our servants, and with it We revive a dead country. Likewise will be the rising [from the dead].
