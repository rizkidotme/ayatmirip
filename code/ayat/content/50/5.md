### بَلْ كَذَّبُوا بِالْحَقِّ لَمَّا جَاءَهُمْ فَهُمْ فِي أَمْرٍ مَرِيجٍ
### meaning: 
Indeed, they denied the truth when it came to them; so they are now in a perplexed state of affairs.
