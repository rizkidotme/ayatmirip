### كَذَّبُوا بِآيَاتِنَا كُلِّهَا فَأَخَذْنَاهُمْ أَخْذَ عَزِيزٍ مُقْتَدِرٍ
### meaning: 
who denied all of Our signs. So We seized them with the seizing of One [who is] all-mighty, Omnipotent.
