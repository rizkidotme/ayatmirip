فَوَقَاهُمُ اللَّهُ شَرَّ ذَٰلِكَ الْيَوْمِ وَلَقَّاهُمْ نَضْرَةً وَسُرُورًا
### meaning: 
So Allah saved them from that day’s ills and graced them with freshness [on this faces] and joy [in their hearts].
