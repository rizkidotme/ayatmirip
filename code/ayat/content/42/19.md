اللَّهُ لَطِيفٌ بِعِبَادِهِ يَرْزُقُ مَنْ يَشَاءُ ۖ وَهُوَ الْقَوِيُّ الْعَزِيزُ
### meaning: 
Allah is all-attentive to His servants. He provides for whomever He wishes, and He is the All-strong, the All-mighty.
