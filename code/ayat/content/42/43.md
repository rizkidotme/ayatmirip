وَلَمَنْ صَبَرَ وَغَفَرَ إِنَّ ذَٰلِكَ لَمِنْ عَزْمِ الْأُمُورِ
### meaning: 
As for someone who endures patiently and forgives—that is indeed the steadiest of courses.
