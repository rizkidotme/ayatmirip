وَجَاءَ فِرْعَوْنُ وَمَنْ قَبْلَهُ وَالْمُؤْتَفِكَاتُ بِالْخَاطِئَةِ
### meaning: 
Then Pharaoh and those who were before him, and the towns that were overturned, brought about iniquity.
