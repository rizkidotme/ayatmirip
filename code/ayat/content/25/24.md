أَصْحَابُ الْجَنَّةِ يَوْمَئِذٍ خَيْرٌ مُسْتَقَرًّا وَأَحْسَنُ مَقِيلًا
### meaning: 
On that day the inhabitants of paradise will be in the best abode and an excellent resting place.
