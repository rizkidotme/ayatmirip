وَلَا يَأْتُونَكَ بِمَثَلٍ إِلَّا جِئْنَاكَ بِالْحَقِّ وَأَحْسَنَ تَفْسِيرًا
### meaning: 
They do not bring you any representation but that We bring you the truth [in reply to them] and the best exposition.
