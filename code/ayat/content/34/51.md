وَلَوْ تَرَىٰ إِذْ فَزِعُوا فَلَا فَوْتَ وَأُخِذُوا مِنْ مَكَانٍ قَرِيبٍ
### meaning: 
Were you to see them when they will be stricken with terror, without any escape, and are seized from a close quarter.
