### لِيَأْكُلُوا مِنْ ثَمَرِهِ وَمَا عَمِلَتْهُ أَيْدِيهِمْ ۖ أَفَلَا يَشْكُرُونَ
### meaning: 
so that they may eat of its fruit and what their hands have cultivated. Will they not then give thanks?
