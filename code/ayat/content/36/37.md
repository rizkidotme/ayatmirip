### وَآيَةٌ لَهُمُ اللَّيْلُ نَسْلَخُ مِنْهُ النَّهَارَ فَإِذَا هُمْ مُظْلِمُونَ
### meaning: 
A sign for them is the night, which We strip of daylight, and, behold, they find themselves in the dark!
