### وَاتَّخَذُوا مِنْ دُونِ اللَّهِ آلِهَةً لَعَلَّهُمْ يُنْصَرُونَ
### meaning: 
They have taken gods besides Allah, [hoping] that they might be helped [by the fake deities].
