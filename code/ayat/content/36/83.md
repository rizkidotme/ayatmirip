### فَسُبْحَانَ الَّذِي بِيَدِهِ مَلَكُوتُ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ وَإِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ
### meaning: 
So immaculate is He in whose hand is the dominion of all things, and to whom you shall be brought back.
