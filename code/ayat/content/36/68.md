### وَمَنْ نُعَمِّرْهُ نُنَكِّسْهُ فِي الْخَلْقِ ۖ أَفَلَا يَعْقِلُونَ
### meaning: 
And whomever We give a long life, We cause him to regress in creation. Then, will they not exercise their reason?
