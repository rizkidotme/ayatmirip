### يُرْسَلُ عَلَيْكُمَا شُوَاظٌ مِنْ نَارٍ وَنُحَاسٌ فَلَا تَنْتَصِرَانِ
### meaning: 
There will be unleashed upon you a flash of fire and a smoke; then you will not be able to help one another.
