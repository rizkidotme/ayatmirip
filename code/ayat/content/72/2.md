### يَهْدِي إِلَى الرُّشْدِ فَآمَنَّا بِهِ ۖ وَلَنْ نُشْرِكَ بِرَبِّنَا أَحَدًا
### meaning: 
which guides to rectitude. Hence, we have believed in it and we will never ascribe any partner to our Lord.
