قَالَ لَهُ مُوسَىٰ هَلْ أَتَّبِعُكَ عَلَىٰ أَنْ تُعَلِّمَنِ مِمَّا عُلِّمْتَ رُشْدًا
### meaning: 
Moses said to him, ‘May I follow you for the purpose that you teach me some of the probity you have been taught?’
