### فَاصْبِرْ إِنَّ وَعْدَ اللَّهِ حَقٌّ ۖ وَلَا يَسْتَخِفَّنَّكَ الَّذِينَ لَا يُوقِنُونَ
### meaning: 
So be patient! Allah’s promise is indeed true. And do not let yourself be upset by those who have no conviction.
