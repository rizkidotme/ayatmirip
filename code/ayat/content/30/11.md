اللَّهُ يَبْدَأُ الْخَلْقَ ثُمَّ يُعِيدُهُ ثُمَّ إِلَيْهِ تُرْجَعُونَ
### meaning: 
Allah originates the creation, then He will bring it back, then you will be brought back to Him.
