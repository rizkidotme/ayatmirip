### مَا قَدَرُوا اللَّهَ حَقَّ قَدْرِهِ ۗ إِنَّ اللَّهَ لَقَوِيٌّ عَزِيزٌ
### meaning: 
They do not regard Allah with the regard due to Him. Indeed Allah is all-strong, all-mighty.
