ذَٰلِكَ وَمَنْ يُعَظِّمْ شَعَائِرَ اللَّهِ فَإِنَّهَا مِنْ تَقْوَى الْقُلُوبِ
### meaning: 
That. And whoever venerates the sacraments of Allah—indeed that arises from the Godwariness of hearts.
