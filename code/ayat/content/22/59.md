### لَيُدْخِلَنَّهُمْ مُدْخَلًا يَرْضَوْنَهُ ۗ وَإِنَّ اللَّهَ لَعَلِيمٌ حَلِيمٌ
### meaning: 
He will admit them into an abode they are pleased with. Indeed Allah is all-knowing, all-forbearing.
