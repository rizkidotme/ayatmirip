### يَدْعُو لَمَنْ ضَرُّهُ أَقْرَبُ مِنْ نَفْعِهِ ۚ لَبِئْسَ الْمَوْلَىٰ وَلَبِئْسَ الْعَشِيرُ
### meaning: 
He invokes someone whose harm is surely likelier than his benefit. An evil ally indeed, and an evil companion!
