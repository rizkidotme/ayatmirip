### وَالَّذِينَ سَعَوْا فِي آيَاتِنَا مُعَاجِزِينَ أُولَٰئِكَ أَصْحَابُ الْجَحِيمِ
### meaning: 
But as for those who contend with Our signs, seeking to frustrate [their purpose], they shall be the inmates of hell.
