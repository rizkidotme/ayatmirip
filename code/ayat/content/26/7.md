### أَوَلَمْ يَرَوْا إِلَى الْأَرْضِ كَمْ أَنْبَتْنَا فِيهَا مِنْ كُلِّ زَوْجٍ كَرِيمٍ
### meaning: 
Have they not regarded the earth, how many of every splendid kind [of vegetation] We have caused to grow in it?
