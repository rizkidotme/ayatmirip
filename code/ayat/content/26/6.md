### فَقَدْ كَذَّبُوا فَسَيَأْتِيهِمْ أَنْبَاءُ مَا كَانُوا بِهِ يَسْتَهْزِئُونَ
### meaning: 
They have already denied [the truth], but soon there will come to them the news of what they have been deriding.
