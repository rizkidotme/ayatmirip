مَنْ جَاءَ بِالْحَسَنَةِ فَلَهُ خَيْرٌ مِنْهَا وَهُمْ مِنْ فَزَعٍ يَوْمَئِذٍ آمِنُونَ
### meaning: 
Whoever brings virtue shall receive [a reward] better than it; and on that day they will be secure from terror.
