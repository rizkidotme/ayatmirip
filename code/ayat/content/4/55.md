### فَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ آمَنَ بِهِ وَمِنْهُمْ مَنْ صَدَّ عَنْهُ ۚ وَكَفَىٰ بِجَهَنَّمَ سَعِيرًا
### meaning: 
Of them are some who believe in him, and of them are some who deter [others] from him; and hell suffices for a blaze!
