### أَمْ لَهُمْ نَصِيبٌ مِنَ الْمُلْكِ فَإِذًا لَا يُؤْتُونَ النَّاسَ نَقِيرًا
### meaning: 
Do they have a share in sovereignty? If so, they will not give those people [so much as] a speck on a date-stone!
