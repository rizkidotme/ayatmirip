### يَعِدُهُمْ وَيُمَنِّيهِمْ ۖ وَمَا يَعِدُهُمُ الشَّيْطَانُ إِلَّا غُرُورًا
### meaning: 
He makes them promises and gives them [false] hopes, yet Satan does not promise them anything but delusion.
