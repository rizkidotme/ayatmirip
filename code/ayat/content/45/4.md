### وَفِي خَلْقِكُمْ وَمَا يَبُثُّ مِنْ دَابَّةٍ آيَاتٌ لِقَوْمٍ يُوقِنُونَ
### meaning: 
There are signs for people who have certainty in your own creation and in animals, He scatters abroad.
