### فَلِلَّهِ الْحَمْدُ رَبِّ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَرَبِّ الْأَرْضِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
### meaning: 
So all praise belongs to Allah, the Lord of the heavens and the Lord of the earth, the Lord of all the worlds.
