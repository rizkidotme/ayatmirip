لِيُحِقَّ الْحَقَّ وَيُبْطِلَ الْبَاطِلَ وَلَوْ كَرِهَ الْمُجْرِمُونَ
### meaning: 
so that He may confirm the truth and bring falsehood to naught, though the guilty should be averse.
