تَنْزِيلُ الْكِتَابِ لَا رَيْبَ فِيهِ مِنْ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
### meaning: 
The [gradual] sending down of the Book, there is no doubt in it, is from the Lord of all the worlds.
