### قَالَ لَمْ أَكُنْ لِأَسْجُدَ لِبَشَرٍ خَلَقْتَهُ مِنْ صَلْصَالٍ مِنْ حَمَإٍ مَسْنُونٍ
### meaning: 
Said he, ‘I will not prostrate before a human whom You have created out of a dry clay [drawn] from an aging mud.’
