### قَالَ أَبَشَّرْتُمُونِي عَلَىٰ أَنْ مَسَّنِيَ الْكِبَرُ فَبِمَ تُبَشِّرُونَ
### meaning: 
He said, ‘Do you give me good news though old age has befallen me? What is the good news that you bring me?’
