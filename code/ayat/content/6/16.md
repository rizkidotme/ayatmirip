### مَنْ يُصْرَفْ عَنْهُ يَوْمَئِذٍ فَقَدْ رَحِمَهُ ۚ وَذَٰلِكَ الْفَوْزُ الْمُبِينُ
### meaning: 
Whoever is spared of it on that day, He has certainly been merciful to him, and that is manifest success.
