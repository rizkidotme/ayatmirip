### وَكَذَّبَ بِهِ قَوْمُكَ وَهُوَ الْحَقُّ ۚ قُلْ لَسْتُ عَلَيْكُمْ بِوَكِيلٍ
### meaning: 
Your people have denied it, though it is the truth. Say, ‘It is not my business to watch over you.’
