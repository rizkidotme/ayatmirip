وَلِكُلٍّ دَرَجَاتٌ مِمَّا عَمِلُوا ۚ وَمَا رَبُّكَ بِغَافِلٍ عَمَّا يَعْمَلُونَ
### meaning: 
For everyone there are ranks in accordance with what they have done; and your Lord is not oblivious of what they do.
