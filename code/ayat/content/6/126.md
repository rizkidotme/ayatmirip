وَهَٰذَا صِرَاطُ رَبِّكَ مُسْتَقِيمًا ۗ قَدْ فَصَّلْنَا الْآيَاتِ لِقَوْمٍ يَذَّكَّرُونَ
### meaning: 
This is the straight path of your Lord. We have already elaborated the signs for a people who take admonition.
