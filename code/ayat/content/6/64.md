### قُلِ اللَّهُ يُنَجِّيكُمْ مِنْهَا وَمِنْ كُلِّ كَرْبٍ ثُمَّ أَنْتُمْ تُشْرِكُونَ
### meaning: 
Say, ‘It is Allah who delivers you from them and from every agony, [but] then you ascribe partners [to Him].’
