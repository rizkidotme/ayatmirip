### قُلْ فَلِلَّهِ الْحُجَّةُ الْبَالِغَةُ ۖ فَلَوْ شَاءَ لَهَدَاكُمْ أَجْمَعِينَ
### meaning: 
Say, ‘To Allah belongs the conclusive argument. Had He wished, He would have surely guided you all.’
