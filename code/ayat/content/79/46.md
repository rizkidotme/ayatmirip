### كَأَنَّهُمْ يَوْمَ يَرَوْنَهَا لَمْ يَلْبَثُوا إِلَّا عَشِيَّةً أَوْ ضُحَاهَا
### meaning: 
The day they see it, it shall be as if they had not stayed [in the world] except for an evening or forenoon.
