### إِنَّ السَّاعَةَ آتِيَةٌ أَكَادُ أُخْفِيهَا لِتُجْزَىٰ كُلُّ نَفْسٍ بِمَا تَسْعَىٰ
### meaning: 
Indeed the Hour is bound to come: I will have it hidden, so that every soul may be rewarded for its endeavour.
