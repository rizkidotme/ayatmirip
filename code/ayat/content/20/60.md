### فَتَوَلَّىٰ فِرْعَوْنُ فَجَمَعَ كَيْدَهُ ثُمَّ أَتَىٰ
### meaning: 
Then Pharaoh withdrew [to consult privately], summoned up his guile, and then arrived [at the scene of the contest].
