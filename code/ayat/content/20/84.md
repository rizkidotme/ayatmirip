قَالَ هُمْ أُولَاءِ عَلَىٰ أَثَرِي وَعَجِلْتُ إِلَيْكَ رَبِّ لِتَرْضَىٰ
### meaning: 
He said, ‘They are close upon my heels, and I hurried on to You, my Lord, that You may be pleased.’
