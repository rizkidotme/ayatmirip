فَأَتْبَعَهُمْ فِرْعَوْنُ بِجُنُودِهِ فَغَشِيَهُمْ مِنَ الْيَمِّ مَا غَشِيَهُمْ
### meaning: 
Then Pharaoh pursued them with his troops, whereat they were engulfed by what engulfed them of the sea.
