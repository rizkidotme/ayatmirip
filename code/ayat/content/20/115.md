وَلَقَدْ عَهِدْنَا إِلَىٰ آدَمَ مِنْ قَبْلُ فَنَسِيَ وَلَمْ نَجِدْ لَهُ عَزْمًا
### meaning: 
Certainly We had enjoined Adam earlier; but he forgot, and We did not find any resoluteness in him.
