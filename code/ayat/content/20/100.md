مَنْ أَعْرَضَ عَنْهُ فَإِنَّهُ يَحْمِلُ يَوْمَ الْقِيَامَةِ وِزْرًا
### meaning: 
Whoever disregards it shall bear a burden [of punishment] on the Day of Resurrection,
