### وَيَسْأَلُونَكَ عَنِ الْجِبَالِ فَقُلْ يَنْسِفُهَا رَبِّي نَسْفًا
### meaning: 
They question you concerning the mountains. Say, ‘My Lord will scatter them [like dust].’
