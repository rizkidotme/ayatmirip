قَالَ عِلْمُهَا عِنْدَ رَبِّي فِي كِتَابٍ ۖ لَا يَضِلُّ رَبِّي وَلَا يَنْسَى
### meaning: 
He said, ‘Their knowledge is with my Lord, in a Book. My Lord neither makes any error nor forgets.’
