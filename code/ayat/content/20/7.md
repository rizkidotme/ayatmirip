### وَإِنْ تَجْهَرْ بِالْقَوْلِ فَإِنَّهُ يَعْلَمُ السِّرَّ وَأَخْفَى
### meaning: 
Whether you speak loudly [or in secret tones,] He indeed knows the secret and what is still more hidden.
