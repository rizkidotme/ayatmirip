### وَلَيَعْلَمَنَّ اللَّهُ الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا وَلَيَعْلَمَنَّ الْمُنَافِقِينَ
### meaning: 
Allah shall surely ascertain those who have faith, and He shall surely ascertain the hypocrites.
