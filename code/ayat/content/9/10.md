### لَا يَرْقُبُونَ فِي مُؤْمِنٍ إِلًّا وَلَا ذِمَّةً ۚ وَأُولَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْمُعْتَدُونَ
### meaning: 
They observe toward a believer neither kinship nor covenant, and it is they who are the transgressors.
