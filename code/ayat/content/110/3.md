فَسَبِّحْ بِحَمْدِ رَبِّكَ وَاسْتَغْفِرْهُ ۚ إِنَّهُ كَانَ تَوَّابًا
### meaning: 
celebrate the praise of your Lord, and plead to Him for forgiveness. Indeed, He is all-clement.
