وَيَا قَوْمِ مَنْ يَنْصُرُنِي مِنَ اللَّهِ إِنْ طَرَدْتُهُمْ ۚ أَفَلَا تَذَكَّرُونَ
### meaning: 
O my people! Who would come to my help against Allah were I to drive them away? Will you not then take admonition?
