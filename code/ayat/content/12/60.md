### فَإِنْ لَمْ تَأْتُونِي بِهِ فَلَا كَيْلَ لَكُمْ عِنْدِي وَلَا تَقْرَبُونِ
### meaning: 
But if you do not bring him to me, then there will be no rations for you with me, and don’t [ever] come near me.’
