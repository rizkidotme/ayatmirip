وَلِلَّهِ مُلْكُ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالْأَرْضِ ۖ وَإِلَى اللَّهِ الْمَصِيرُ
### meaning: 
To Allah belongs the kingdom of the heavens and the earth, and toward Allah is the destination.
