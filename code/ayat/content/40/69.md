### أَلَمْ تَرَ إِلَى الَّذِينَ يُجَادِلُونَ فِي آيَاتِ اللَّهِ أَنَّىٰ يُصْرَفُونَ
### meaning: 
Have you not regarded those who dispute the signs of Allah, where they are being led away [from Allah’s way]?
