### تَحِيَّتُهُمْ يَوْمَ يَلْقَوْنَهُ سَلَامٌ ۚ وَأَعَدَّ لَهُمْ أَجْرًا كَرِيمًا
### meaning: 
The day they encounter Him, their greeting will be, ‘Peace,’ and He holds in store for them a noble reward.
