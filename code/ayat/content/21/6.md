### مَا آمَنَتْ قَبْلَهُمْ مِنْ قَرْيَةٍ أَهْلَكْنَاهَا ۖ أَفَهُمْ يُؤْمِنُونَ
### meaning: 
No town that We destroyed before them believed. Will these then have faith [if they are sent signs]?
