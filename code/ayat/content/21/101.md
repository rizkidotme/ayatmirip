### إِنَّ الَّذِينَ سَبَقَتْ لَهُمْ مِنَّا الْحُسْنَىٰ أُولَٰئِكَ عَنْهَا مُبْعَدُونَ
### meaning: 
Indeed those to whom there has gone beforehand [the promise of] the best reward from Us will be kept away from it.
