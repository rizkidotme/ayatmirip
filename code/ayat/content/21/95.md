وَحَرَامٌ عَلَىٰ قَرْيَةٍ أَهْلَكْنَاهَا أَنَّهُمْ لَا يَرْجِعُونَ
### meaning: 
It is forbidden for [the people of] any town that We have destroyed [to return to the world]: they shall not return,
