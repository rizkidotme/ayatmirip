### فَذَاقَتْ وَبَالَ أَمْرِهَا وَكَانَ عَاقِبَةُ أَمْرِهَا خُسْرًا
### meaning: 
So it tasted the evil consequences of its conduct, and the outcome of its conduct was ruin.
