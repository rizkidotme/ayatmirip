### فَلَنَسْأَلَنَّ الَّذِينَ أُرْسِلَ إِلَيْهِمْ وَلَنَسْأَلَنَّ الْمُرْسَلِينَ
### meaning: 
We will surely question those to whom the apostles were sent, and We will surely question the apostles.
