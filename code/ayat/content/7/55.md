### ادْعُوا رَبَّكُمْ تَضَرُّعًا وَخُفْيَةً ۚ إِنَّهُ لَا يُحِبُّ الْمُعْتَدِينَ
### meaning: 
Supplicate your Lord, beseechingly and secretly. Indeed, He does not like the transgressors.
