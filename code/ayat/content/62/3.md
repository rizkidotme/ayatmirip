### وَآخَرِينَ مِنْهُمْ لَمَّا يَلْحَقُوا بِهِمْ ۚ وَهُوَ الْعَزِيزُ الْحَكِيمُ
### meaning: 
And to others from among them [as well] who have not yet joined them. And He is the All-mighty, the All-wise.
