خَالِدِينَ فِيهَا ۖ لَا يُخَفَّفُ عَنْهُمُ الْعَذَابُ وَلَا هُمْ يُنْظَرُونَ
### meaning: 
They will remain in it [forever] and their punishment shall not be lightened, nor will they be granted any respite.
